Fun Betta Fish Facts

🧠 Intelligence

Betta fish can recognize their owners and have memories that can last up to 3 months!

🌈 Colors

They change colors based on mood and can display a full spectrum including blues, reds, greens, and oranges.

💪 Fighters

Male bettas are highly territorial and will aggressively defend their space against other males.

🫁 Breathing

Bettas can breathe air from the surface! They have a special organ called a labyrinth that allows this.
